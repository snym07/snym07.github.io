15 Facts about Dark Web

1) Tor

The Dark web can only be accessed using the software Tor which provides anonymity to the user.

2) Size

It is impossible to measure the size of the Deep Web because the majority of the information is hidden or locked inside databases. Early estimates suggested that the deep web is 400 to 550 times larger than the surface web (deep web contains 7500 TB of info as compared to 19 TB of surface web).

3) Banned Books

You can read banned books, journals, articles etc on the Dark web.

4) Fake ID

There are people who can steal credit card informations for the right amount on the dark web. People can even purchase fake identifications too.

5) Hacking

You can find people willing to hack anything, from your girlfriend's facebook account to government's official websites.

6) Weapons

Illegal weapons are sold on the Dark web and you can even buy Drugs from the websites like Silkroad (recently shut down by FBI).

7) Extremists

It is estimated that more than 50,000 extremist websites are exist on Deep web.

8) Hitmen

Hitmen could be hired from the Dark web to kill anybody from an ordinary person (20,000 euro) to High rank government Officials (100,000 euro).

9) Bitcoins

Bit-coins is the dominent currency on the Dark web (1 bitcoin equals 229.14 US Dollar - at the time of writing).

10) Child porn

An overwhelming majority of Dark web's traffic is driven by the sexual abuse of children. 7axxn is a pedophiles (people who are sexually attracted to children) community of "over 90,000" registered users, filled with gigabyte after gigabyte of child pornography.

11) Human Experiments

There are experiments held on real human subjects on Dark web, experiments include starvation and fluid restriction, vivisection, infectious diseases, drug trials, sterilization and neonatal and infant tolerances to various things.

12) Cicada 3301

The series of puzzles collectively known as Cicada 3301 was held on the Deep web.

13) Child spirits

You can purchase child spirits from abortions and miscarriages and even ghosts/devas for just 120 USD.

14) Snuff movies

On Dark web you can find Snuff movies (Snuff Movies are movies that involve actual murdering or mutilation of people for entertainment or sexual gratification purposes).

15) Sad-satan

There is a terrifying game called "sad-satan" featuring audio clips from the likes of Charles Manson and imagery of Hitler, the game utilizes cheap real-life creepiness but compounds it with a miasma of unanswered questions. The game has the player walking down strange hallways and unfocused paths in a vortex, includes coded messages and Chinese childrens songs.

A youtube channel "Obscure Horror Corner" found this game and published the footage recorded of the game in five videos, OHC host Jamie Farrell deleted the game after it allegedly started threatening him.
