Looking at human brain from the perspective of a programmer

Artificial Intelligence 24th Jan 2017

Artificial Intelligence is a hot topic right now. Many great minds are working and researching in this rapidly growing field of computers and machines. The very basic idea of the Artificial Intelligence came from the human brain itself. If we look at the the human brain then it is the most powerful thing capable of creating, building, destroying, innovating and discovering things.

Our brain consists of two parts :
1) Conscious and
2) Unconscious
In the conscious part we are able to control our senses and do whatever we want to do like jogging, playing, making choices etc. Whereas the unconscious part controls the blood flow, beating of heart, digestion of food and every other thing of our body which is not under control of our ability of making choices. If we think all the activities as processes and those processes as simple programme (just like a computer programme) then everything our conscious mind do is a choice of selecting a list of programmes from our body which includes even the slightest movement of our hand from complex movements likes playing football and every programme which is responsible for the basics of living like flow of blood are govern and automatically run by the unconscious mind.

This observation can help us understanding the intelligence possessed by us humans and potentially helping us to be able to create some Artificial Intelligence to execute programmes just in the same way we humans do. Obviously the complexity of human body is extraordinary and even the modern sensors are not as much capable of sensing and performing the tasks that our body does.
